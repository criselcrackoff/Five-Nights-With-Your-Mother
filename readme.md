# Five Nights With Your Mother (FNWYM)

**Five Nights With Your Mother (FNWYM)** is a Python UCN fangame based where you fend off plushies? drawings? ...anyway! FNWYM is based off **Ultimate Custom Night (UCN)** and the **Five Nights at Freddy's Series.**

Instead of surviving against haunted animatronics, you'll be defending yourself from an absurd cast of plushies, drawings, and other bizarre characters, each with their own unique mechanics. Every enemy behaves differently, forcing you to learn their patterns and adapt your strategy.


## Gameplay

Like **Ultimate Custom Night**, FNWYM allows you to completely customize your experience.

- Choose which enemies will appear.
- Set each character's AI level individually.
- Create your own custom challenges.
- Test yourself with increasingly difficult nights.


Unlike UCN, FNWYM also lets you customize **how long the night lasts**, allowing for both quick sessions and endurance challenges.

## Night Lengths

The game currently features four selectable night lengths:


### Normal
The standard experience.

- Time: **12:00 AM – 6:00 AM**
- Real duration: **7.5 minutes**

### Extended
Two extra in-game hours.

- Time: **12:00 AM – 8:00 AM**
- Real duration: **10 minutes**

### XL
Start earlier and survive even longer.

- Time: **10:00 PM – 8:00 AM**
- Real duration: **12.5 minutes**

### XXL
The ultimate endurance challenge.

- Time: **8:00 PM – 8:00 AM**
- Real duration: **15 minutes**

About:

Five Nights With Your Mother (FNWYM) is a Python UCN fangame based where you fend off plushies? drawings? ...anyway! FNWYM is based off Ultimate Custom Night (UCN), a mode where you choose how hard the night will be, how many animatronics will be in the night in a FNaF-style gamplay! UCN also comes with prefixed challenges that reward you with their completion. In this case you'll be choosing not only how many animatronics you're going to fend off? but also determain how long the night last!


The night has 4 lengths each during more or less time.

Normal: this is the lowest you can go, survive till 6 AM on a 12 to 6 AM shift. (7,5 min)

Extended: The night will have 2 extra hours, survive till 8 AM. (10 min)

XL: The night will start earlier at 10 PM, survive till 8 AM. (12,5 min)

XXL: The night will start earlier a 8 PM, can you survive till 8 AM in a 12 in-game hours? (15 min)
